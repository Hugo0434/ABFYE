About the Competition

Our soccer robot competition – “Bright Future Cup” is co-organized by The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, EDB STEM Education Centre and Art & Technology Education Centre. The aim is to encourage the participants to use their STEAM knowledge in the competition. This is so that their creativity, innovation, teamworking and problem-solving skillsets can be nurtured.
